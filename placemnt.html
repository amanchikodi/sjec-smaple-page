About Placement


Industry interaction with the Department enhances students internship, consultancy projects and placements. Students are trained under I-Point, which helps in placements and highly focus on the overall Personality Development of every individual. The Placement Training Activities are also conducted for the students by the Departmental staffs to qualify them for placements.

There are numerous opportunities to participate in projects and postgraduate research. There is no shortage of exciting career opportunities available to graduates.
